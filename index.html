Scope of Work

Everything Included

One flat rate covers the entire production. From script refinement to final 4K delivery, every step is handled so you can focus on what you do best.

Script Refinement and Interview Framework +
We refine your talking points into a structured 9-section interview framework. Each section is designed to flow naturally while covering everything your audience needs to hear, from your origin story to your process, certifications, and the science behind what makes your work different.
B-Roll Shot List and Production Planning +
A detailed shot list covering staff operations, equipment staging, SPC process, containment workflow, and R-Max demo. Every visual is mapped out before the cameras roll so we maximize the half-day shoot window.
Half-Day Production Shoot (Up to 6 Hours) +
Full on-location production including setup, lighting, audio, and multi-angle camera work. The day is split between a 60-90 minute founder interview and 2-3 hours of b-roll capture. We run scripted and conversational passes to get the most authentic delivery.
Founder Interview (9 Content Sections) +
The centerpiece of the video. Nine structured sections, each designed to build trust and authority with your target audience. We capture both scripted responses and natural conversation to build a final cut that feels genuine and authoritative.

The 9 Sections:
1. Your Origin Story
2. The Problem: Why Mold and Small Particles Matter
3. Your Medical-Grade Approach
4. Small Particle Cleaning: The Science
5. Equipment and Technology (R-Max, Containment Systems)
6. Certifications and Industry Standards
7. Working with Functional Medicine Practitioners and IEPs
8. Results: A Real Project Walkthrough
9. How to Work with Restoration Medics
B-Roll Capture +
2-3 hours of cinematic b-roll: your team in action, equipment close-ups, containment setup and teardown, the SPC process, R-Max demonstration, and environmental shots. These visuals layer over the interview to show your audience what they are investing in.
Professional Editing and Assembly +
Your footage is edited into a polished 4-6 minute video. We select the strongest interview takes, weave in b-roll, and build a narrative arc that holds attention from the first frame to the last. Two revision rounds are included: rough cut and fine cut.
Color Correction, Audio Cleanup, and Music +
Professional color grading for a consistent, cinematic look. Audio is cleaned, leveled, and mixed with licensed background music that complements your brand tone without overpowering the interview.
Lower Thirds, Text Overlays, and Branded Graphics +
Name titles, section headers, key statistics, and your logo are designed into the video with clean, on-brand motion graphics. Every text element uses your brand typography and color system.
Final Delivery: 4K MP4 (3840x2160, H.264) +
The finished video is delivered as a broadcast-quality 4K file in 16:9 aspect ratio. Ready for expo booth displays, your website, email campaigns, BNI presentations, and practitioner outreach. One file. Every use case.
